Welcome to SCT!

Welcome to Thomas Jefferson's Senior Computer Team! The Senior Computer Team, or SCT, meets every Friday A-block. Our objective is to practice and enhance problem-solving skills involving programming. We compete in several contests, including USACO, Codeforces, and ACM on the national and international level. Please come by!
